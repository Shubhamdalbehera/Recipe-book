Chawal

prep time

10 min.

cook time

10 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : rice chawal brown rice carbs

instructions

step 1

Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Drain in a colander and set aside. In a medium saucepan, bring the water to a boil. Add the salt, stir, and then add the rinsed and drained rice. Stir with a fork.

step 2

Reduce the heat to low, cover, and let simmer for 20 minutes. Check it after 15 minutes to see if all the water has evaporated. If it has, the rice is ready. If not, replace the lid and let the rice simmer an additional 5 minutes

step 3

Remove from the heat and fluff with a fork. Transfer to a plate and serve garnished with butter and parsley, if desired.

ingredients

1 cup long-grain white rice

2 cups water

1/2 teaspoon salt

1 tablespoon unsalted butter, optional

1 tablespoon finely chopped parsley, optional
